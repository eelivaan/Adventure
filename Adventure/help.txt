Useful keys:

[Enter]       Submit command
[Arrow Up]    Scroll through previous commands
[Arrow Down]  Scroll through previous commands
[Esc]         Exit application
[Ctrl]        Retreat quickly (reverse ongoing movement)


Available commands:

Movement:
go left
go right
go up
go down

Restart the game:
restart

Use a key, if you have found one, to unlock a door:
use key

Additionally, when there's some sort of question on screen,
you just type your answer to it (just one noun or a code).
